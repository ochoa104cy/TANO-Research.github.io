# TANO Research — Central Hub

> **TANO Research** stands for **Trust, Accuracy, Neutrality, Organization, and Transparency.**  
> This is the unified gateway connecting all TANO initiatives in cybersecurity, data science, and AI governance.

---

## Overview

TANO Research is a multidisciplinary framework and ecosystem designed to advance open-source research, automate compliance, and foster trustworthy AI.  
The platform integrates cybersecurity, compliance, and AI research into one cohesive network.

---

## Core Pillars

| Project | Description | 
|----------|--------------|
| **TANO Framework** | The conceptual and methodological foundation that unifies cybersecurity and data science disciplines. 
| **TANO + CMMC Playbook** | Full implementation guide and mapping of NIST SP 800-171 → CMMC Level 1 and 2 with AI-based compliance assistance. 
| **TANO-Cyber** | Toolkits, scripts, and lab environments for cybersecurity testing, analysis, and automation. 
| **TANO-AI** | Applied AI for compliance, threat detection, and cognitive analytics.

---

## Mission

- Advance **cybersecurity assurance** through open frameworks.  
- Bridge **data science + AI ethics** with compliance automation.  
- Support **research reproducibility and transparency**.  
- Empower **students, professionals, and enterprises** with accessible, verifiable tools.

---

## Features

- Unified compliance dashboards (CMMC v3, NIST 800-171)  
- AI-assisted risk & evidence interpretation  
- Secure configuration and lab automation (macOS / Windows / Kali)  
- Knowledge engine integrating documentation + datasets  
- Public + private deployment options (GitHub Pages / Cloud)

---

## Framework Philosophy

> **TANO = Trust × Accuracy × Neutrality × Organization × Transparency**

| Principle | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Trust** | Built through integrity, open review, and verifiable evidence. |
| **Accuracy** | Quantitative validation using reproducible methods. |
| **Neutrality** | Framework design remains non-biased and universally adaptable. |
| **Organization** | Structured documentation, taxonomies, and architecture. |
| **Transparency** | Open publication, traceable provenance, and accountability. |
