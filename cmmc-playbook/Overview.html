CMMC Playbook Overview

TANO = Trust • Accuracy • Neutrality • Organization • Transparency

This overview introduces the purpose, scope, and structure of the TANO CMMC Playbook.

Purpose

The TANO CMMC Playbook provides a practical, organized, and accessible guide for implementing CMMC Level 1 (Foundational) and preparing for Level 2 (Advanced) requirements. The Playbook is intentionally structured for small/medium organizations, academic labs, and training environments.

What’s Included

  • CMMC Level 1 practice summaries
  • Templates for SSP, POA&M, and Risk Register
  • Evidence guidance and examples
  • Directory structure for Level 2 expansion
  • Diagrams to visualize systems and CUI boundaries

Roadmap

  1. Phase 1 – Level 1 Foundations
    Complete L1 summaries, finalize templates, add diagrams, and create a clear website hub.
  2. Phase 2 – Level 2 Expansion
    Add NIST 800-171 mapping, policy library, scoring method, and assessment flow.
  3. Phase 3 – Automation
    Add scripts for logging, asset inventory, user access reviews, and evidence collection.

The Playbook is designed for continuous improvement and future integration with dashboards, AI-supported analysis and risk monitoring tools under the TANO Research ecosystem.
