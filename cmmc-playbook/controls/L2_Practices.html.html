CMMC Level 2 Practices (Draft Structure)

TANO = Trust • Accuracy • Neutrality • Organization • Transparency

Level 2 aligns with the 110 controls from NIST SP 800-171. This page provides a structured, human-readable way to organize those practices for future expansion.

Example: Access Control (AC)

Level 2 introduces more detailed access control expectations, including session handling, account management, and enforcement of least privilege.

AC.1.001 / AC.L1-3.1.1 – Authorized Access

Baseline from Level 1: only authorized users, processes, and devices can access systems.

AC.2.007 – Least Privilege

Ensure that users are granted the minimum access needed for their role, and that elevated access is time-bound and monitored.

AC.2.013 – Session Lock

Configure session timeouts and automatic screen locks on endpoints that process CUI.

Example: Audit & Accountability (AU)

AU.2.041 – Audit Logging

Enable logging for key systems (e.g., authentication, VPN, privileged actions) and retain logs long enough to support investigations.

AU.2.042 – Review & Analyze Logs

Perform periodic log reviews or use automated tools/SIEM to detect suspicious behavior.

Planned Structure

  • Planned / existing controls
