TANO - CMMC Level 1 / Level 2 Implementation Playbook

Trust * Accuracy * Neutrality * Organization * Transparency

The TANO CMMC Playbook is a lightweight, practical guide designed to help small business and academic teams quickly understand and implement the core requirements of the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC).

Purpose:
This Playbook gives organizations a clear, step-by-step roadmap for meeting CMMC Level 1 (Foundational) and preparing for Level 2 (Advanced) requirements.

It is built to be:
    * Easy to understand
    * Actionable
    * Aligned with NIST SP 800-171
    *Based on real small business environments
    *Modular so teams can adopt controls at their own pace
